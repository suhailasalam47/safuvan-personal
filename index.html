About Me

Get to Know Me

I'm an electrical engineer with roots in the vibrant city of Calicut, Kerala. Growing up in this beautiful coastal city, I was always fascinated by the mechanics of how things work, which led me to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering from APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.
After completing my studies, I began my career in my hometown, working for a year to gain hands-on experience and build my skills. My journey took an exciting turn when I moved to Dubai, where I worked as an MEP engineer for two years. This experience broadened my perspective and exposed me to diverse challenges and opportunities in the field.

I later joined another esteemed company in Dubai as an electrical engineer, which proved to be a significant turning point in my career. I became involved in several groundbreaking projects that pushed the boundaries of innovation and technical excellence. Currently, I am proud to be part of a major project in Azerbaijan, where I am contributing my expertise to a globally significant initiative. Passionate and dedicated to my field, I thrive on solving complex problems and am committed to making a meaningful impact through my work. I believe in the power of continuous learning and collaboration, and I'm always eager to take on new challenges that help drive progress.
